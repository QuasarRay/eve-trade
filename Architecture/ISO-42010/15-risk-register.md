## Risk Review Rule

Every open risk must either be accepted by the owning stakeholder, mitigated by
implementation or operations work, or explicitly deferred with a review date.

No risk in this register is currently accepted for untrusted production
exposure. `Open production blocker` risks must be closed or explicitly accepted
by the listed acceptance authority before production readiness can be claimed.
